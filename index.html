About Me

As a seventh-semester student in the Geographical Information Systems Study Program, Vocational School, Universitas Gadjah Mada, I have strong interest and expertise in spatial data management and analysis. With experience using various GIS software such as ArcGIS, AutoCAD, QGIS, pgAdmin, and Ocean Data View, I am capable of processing and visualizing spatial data effectively. I also have basic knowledge of several programming languages such as R, Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and PHP, which I use for spatial data management, analysis, WebGIS development, and application building. Additionally, I am skilled in deep learning-based data processing using Google Earth Engine (GEE) and ArcGIS Pro, particularly for object detection, feature extraction, and 3D modeling, enabling more accurate and efficient geospatial-based decision-making.
